> Abdukarimov, A. A. (2026). *Fractal Metascience: Foundations and a Research
> Programme for Recursive, Multi-Scale, Human-AI Symbiotic Knowledge Systems.*

Documents in this repository are **archival versions** developed during 2024–2025.
They contain terminology, formulations, and case studies that have been revised
in the 2026 canonical article. Specifically:

- **Quantum terminology** (quantum-semantic, quantum superposition, quantum
  coherence applied to cognition or documents): treated as analogy or design
  vocabulary in the canonical version, not as validated physical claims.
- **Illustrative case studies with statistical results** (e.g. Part IV/V of
  the monograph): these are worked examples, not completed empirical studies.
  The 2026 article replaces them with a proposed research programme (§9).
- **"SIMULATION MODE" or "quantum superposition active" headers**: artifacts
  of an early drafting convention, superseded by the reality-based approach.
